OUR STORY

Crafting Spaces That Tell Your Story

Founded in 2015, Kala Vihaan & Associates has been at the forefront of interior design excellence in Bhopal. What started as a passion project by two visionary designers has grown into one of Central India's most trusted design studios. We believe that every space has a story waiting to be told, and our role is to bring that narrative to life through thoughtful design, premium materials, and meticulous craftsmanship.

With over 7 years of experience, we have successfully transformed hundreds of residential and commercial spaces, creating environments that seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality. Our approach is deeply personal — we listen, understand, and then create spaces that are uniquely yours.

Our Vision

To redefine luxury living in India by creating spaces that are not just beautiful but deeply functional and sustainable. We envision a world where every home and commercial space tells a unique story.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the philosophy of 'less is more, but better.' Every element we introduce into a space must serve both an aesthetic and functional purpose. We combine traditional Indian design sensibilities with contemporary global trends.

OUR TEAM

Meet the Founders

Piyush Tilwani

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

With a degree in Interior Architecture and over a decade of experience, Piyush brings a unique blend of contemporary aesthetics and functional design to every project. His keen eye for detail and passion for innovative materials have earned him recognition in the design community.

HOW WE WORK

Design Process

1. Discovery

We begin with understanding your vision, lifestyle, and requirements through detailed consultations.

2. Concept Development

Our team creates mood boards, sketches, and 3D visualizations to bring your ideas to life.

3. Design & Detailing

Every element is carefully detailed — from material selection to furniture layout to lighting design.

4. Execution

Our turnkey execution team brings the design to reality with precision craftsmanship.

5. Handover

We ensure every detail is perfect before handing over your dream space.
